Export analytics data from your CloudShare BI dashboards as CSV files - straight from your browser.

How it works
1

Drag the button to your bookmarks bar

Press Ctrl+Shift+B to show your bookmarks bar if hidden, then drag the orange button below into it.

2

Go to CloudShare Analytics

Navigate to any dashboard on bi.cloudshare.com. If you click the bookmarklet from another CloudShare page, it redirects you automatically.

3

Click the bookmarklet and choose your datasets

An overlay appears with all 7 datasets. Check the ones you want, click Export, and the CSVs download directly to your machine.

FAQ
Does this send data to an external server?
No. The bookmarklet runs entirely in your browser on bi.cloudshare.com. All queries go directly to Sisense's internal JAQL API using your existing session. CSVs are built and downloaded locally.
What if I click it on the wrong page?
If you're on another CloudShare page, it redirects you to bi.cloudshare.com automatically. Click the bookmarklet again once you're there.
Chrome is blocking multiple downloads?
Chrome may ask permission to download multiple files. Click "Allow" when prompted. This only happens the first time.
How many rows does it pull?
Up to 50,000 rows per dataset. For most CloudShare accounts this covers everything.
How long does it take?
Usually 15-45 seconds depending on how many datasets you select. They run one at a time to avoid overloading Sisense.
