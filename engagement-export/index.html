Engagement Export

Export student tab engagement data from your CloudShare training experiences to CSV — in one click.

Setup
1

Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible

Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+B  ·  Safari: View → Show Favorites Bar
2

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar to install

CS Engagement Export
Drag & drop onto your bookmarks bar
How to Use
3

Log into accelerate.cloudshare.com or use.cloudshare.com

4

Click the CS Engagement Export bookmark in your bookmarks bar

5

Select your date range and filters, then click Export to CSV

6

Your CSV file will download automatically when the export completes

Features
Platforms

15 parallel requests
Filtering
Date range picker

CSV Output
training_name class_id experience_id platform kind status start_date student_name student_email date tab_name active_seconds active_minutes

Large accounts with 1,000+ classes may take 3–5 minutes. Keep the tab open while the export runs. Open the developer console (F12) to view detailed progress.
