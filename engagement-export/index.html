CloudShare Engagement Export

One-click export of student tab engagement data to CSV. Works on Classic and Accelerate.

Install (one time)

1

Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+B)

2

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

📊 CS Export

How to use

3

Log into accelerate.cloudshare.com or use.cloudshare.com

4

Click the "CS Export" bookmark

5

Pick date range (optional), check filters, click Export to CSV

6

Wait for the progress bar to fill — CSV downloads automatically

Large accounts: 1000+ classes may take 3-5 minutes. Don't close the tab while it's running. Open the console (F12) to see detailed progress.
