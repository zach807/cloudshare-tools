Common questions
Does this send data to an external server?
No. Everything runs in your browser. The CSV files are generated locally and downloaded directly to your machine.
Do I need to install anything?
No. It's a browser bookmark. Drag the button to your bookmarks bar once, then click it whenever you need an export.
Which browsers are supported?
Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. Safari may work but isn't officially tested.
How long does an export take?
Depends on the number of classes and students. A typical export of 50 classes finishes in under a minute. The overlay shows progress as it runs.
Can I export just a few specific classes?
Yes. The class filter lets you search and select individual classes by name.
