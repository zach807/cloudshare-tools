Engagement Export Tool

Export student engagement data from CloudShare with full analytics, smart filtering, and one-click download.

Install
1

Show your bookmarks bar

Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) to toggle it on.

2

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar

Click and drag the orange button below into your bookmarks bar.

CS Engagement Export

Then navigate to your CloudShare account and click the bookmark to open the export panel.

3

Use it

Go to use.cloudshare.com or your Accelerate portal, then click the bookmark. Set your filters and export.

Features

CSV Export

Multi-file ZIP with daily detail, student summary, and metadata

Student Summary

Aggregated stats per student: total time, active days, date range

Smart Filtering

Filter by date, status, class name, minimum students

Both Platforms

Works on Classic and Accelerate CloudShare accounts

Progress & ETA

Real-time progress bar with estimated time remaining

Session Check

Detects expired sessions before wasting your time

How it works

This tool runs entirely in your browser using your existing CloudShare session. It reads engagement data from CloudShare's APIs and generates downloadable CSV files locally. No data is sent to any external server. No extensions or installs required.

Version 10 • Chrome, Edge, Firefox • Requires active CloudShare login
